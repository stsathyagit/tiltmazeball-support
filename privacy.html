Tilt Maze Ball Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Tilt Maze Ball ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the App.

Information Collection

Tilt Maze Ball does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personally identifiable information.

The App may use third-party services that collect information used to improve functionality, provide advertisements, and analyze usage.

Advertising

Tilt Maze Ball uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect and process data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy, including device identifiers, advertising identifiers, diagnostics information, and interaction data.

Google Privacy Policy

Analytics and Diagnostics

The App may collect anonymous technical information such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Crash reports
  • Performance diagnostics

This information is used solely to improve the stability and performance of the App.

Children's Privacy

Tilt Maze Ball does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Storage

Game progress, unlocked skins, diamonds, settings, and player preferences are stored locally on the user's device.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services including:

  • Apple App Store Services
